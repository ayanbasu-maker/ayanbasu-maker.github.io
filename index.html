01. About Me

I'm a product leader who thrives at the intersection of technology, strategy, and people. My career has taken me from semiconductor fabs at Samsung to the forefront of AI-powered SaaS platforms — and I've loved every pivot along the way.

I specialize in taking products from 0-to-1 and scaling mature platforms alike. Whether it's launching an AI-native data platform, driving product-led growth with enterprise partners, or building a product org from the ground up, I focus on unlocking revenue and transforming customer experience.
